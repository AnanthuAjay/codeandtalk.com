The objective is to provide a place where Open Source conference organizers can easily list their events (it is just a pull-request) and where they can check what date might fit in the more generic schedule of Open Source and Tech oriented conferences.

For potential speakers we also include the deadline for submitting talk proposals.

Partially related: I also run the Code-Maven Open Source podcast and video interview series.

Sources

There are plenty of places where events are collected. Some are generic places where any type of event can be listed. Other are for a specific technology or a specific country.
  • Eventbrite list of conferences and other events in any subject.
  • Meetup is for any kind of meeting, not necessarily tech and not necessarily conference.
  • Linux Foundation Events. Events organized by the Linux Foundation
  • GoLang conferences
  • Drupalcon
  • DroidCon Android related events.
  • PHP Conferences
  • The Society of Digital Information and Wireless Communications (SDIWC)
  • IT events in Bulgaria (in Bulgarian)
  • Open Source calendar (but it usually only lists for the next 2 months, not good for call for papers)
  • Tech conf aggregators by @CallbackWomen
  • Call for Papers
