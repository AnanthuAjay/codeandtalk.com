Is this real life? Given the growth in maturity of Virtual Reality (VR) technology, this talk aims to give a conceptual overview of emerging human-virtual interactions, and insights into a new world of research and problem solving. It is also and an introduction to the various technical options for developing VR software and interactions with JavaScript.
