Factors for effective mentorship

Mentor:
  • Evaluate your motivation
  • An honest assessment of your time, talent, and temperament.
  • Set realistic goals
Mentee:
  • Reciprocity
  • Respect for mentoring relationship
  • Identified desired outcomes
As a mentor you need Patience, free time, and knowledge of the subject matter.
