Debian is one of the largest and oldest Free Operating Systems. The Debian Perl Group makes CPAN distributions available to users of Debian and its derivates as Debian packages. This talk gives an overview of the group's work, and presents some ideas to the Perl community how CPAN authors can make our work even easier.
