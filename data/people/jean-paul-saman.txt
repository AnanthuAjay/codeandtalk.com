Jean-Paul Saman is a consultant, senior software architect and engineer since 1994 with experience in developing multimedia, embedded and networking systems.
He has worked for various companies in Research and Development jobs as well as in productizing software.

Furthermore he actively contributes to Open Source Software projects such as: "Das Universal bootloader" (u-boot), VideoLAN project and sometimes the Linux Kernel.
